Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, PhysicsHub is a registered nonprofit educational organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

How is my donation used?

Approximately 85% of donations go directly to content development (simulations, videos, and educational materials), 10% to platform maintenance and improvements, and 5% to administrative costs.

Can I donate if I'm outside the United States?

Yes, we accept international donations through our secure payment processors. Currency conversion is handled automatically.

How do I cancel my monthly donation?

You can cancel or modify your monthly donation at any time by logging into your account and visiting the 'Donation Settings' page.
